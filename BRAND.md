# Brand Usage

PrimeTask is a product of TaskCore LTD.

This repository's open-source license applies to the code in this repository.

It does **not** grant rights to use PrimeTask branding in a way that suggests official status, endorsement, or origin beyond accurate descriptive use.

## PrimeTask name and logo

The `PrimeTask` name, logos, and official branding are used by TaskCore LTD for PrimeTask and its official distributions.

Compatible forks and modified builds may describe themselves in truthful, non-confusing terms such as:

- `Compatible with PrimeTask`
- `Fork of the PrimeTask Obsidian plugin`

They must not present themselves as:

- the official PrimeTask plugin
- an endorsed PrimeTask release
- a PrimeTask build published by TaskCore LTD when that is not true

## Official builds

PrimeTask may use signed builds and in-app trust indicators to distinguish official or trusted plugin builds from unsigned or modified ones.

## Brand status

PrimeTask branding is used here as a source identifier for the product and its official distributions. Nothing in this repository should be read as granting independent branding rights to third parties.
